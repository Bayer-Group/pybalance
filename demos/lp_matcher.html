Note that it is possible for the ‚Äúbalance‚Äù value to go up (hopefully only slightly) as the solver progresses. This behavior is due to rounding errors incurred when casting a continuous problem into the discrete space understood by the solver. The ‚Äúbalance‚Äù reported here is the actual balance computed to full accuracy on the original dataset; the ‚Äúobjective‚Äù value here is the actual quantity being optimized and should never increase.
